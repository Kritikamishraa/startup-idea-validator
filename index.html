FAQ

-

What is Startup Validator and who is it for?

Startup Validator is an AI-powered tool that evaluates business ideas for entrepreneurs, students, and professionals, helping them understand their idea’s potential before investing time and money.

+

Is my startup idea safe and confidential?

Yes. All your ideas are securely processed and never shared with any third party. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

+

How does Startup Validator analyze my idea?

You simply enter your idea and choose your industry. Our AI checks market trends, competition, and growth potential to give you a viability score and actionable recommendations.

+

Can Startup Validator guarantee success?

No tool can guarantee success, but Startup Validator helps you avoid common mistakes and provides insights to improve your chances of building a successful startup.

+

Do I need technical or business experience to use it?

Not at all. Startup Validator is designed for everyone—just describe your idea in simple words and get instant feedback.
